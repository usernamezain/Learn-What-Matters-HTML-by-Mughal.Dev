Semantic Article Structure

<article>
    <header>
        <h2>AI Revolution</h2>
        <p>By <address>John Doe</address></p>
    </header>
    
    <section>
        <h3>Introduction</h3>
        <p>Current state of AI...</p>
    </section>
    
    <figure>
        <img src="ai.jpg" alt="AI graphic">
        <figcaption>AI technology visualization</figcaption>
    </figure>
    
    <footer>
        <p>Posted on <time>2023-09-01</time></p>
    </footer>
</article>

Semantic Text Elements

The <mark> tag highlights important text.
Use <time> for dates: Sep 1st.
<cite> marks The Great Gatsby as a reference.
<abbr> explains WHO abbreviations.

Accessibility Benefits

Screen Reader Output:
  • "Navigation landmark" for <nav>
  • "Main landmark" for <main>
  • "Article" announced for <article>
  • "Banner landmark" for <header>
